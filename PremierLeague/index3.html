History of Winners of English Football League

The animated viz shows how teams compete over the English Football League from 1888 till 2018. The first in rank wins the league title. The winner gets the most collected points. if the points are the same, then the goal difference should break the tie.

The animated viz can be controlled to narrow down the time period. Move the slider, and then press the play button to watch how close the first and the second are from each other every season.
